How CredentIQ Works

Our advanced Passive Aggressive Classifier model processes data in real-time through a 3-tier pipeline.

01
📝

Data Input & Cleaning

User submits an article. Our system immediately strips special characters, URLs, and normalizes the text using RegEx.

02
🔢

TF-IDF Vectorization

The cleaned text is converted into a matrix of TF-IDF numerical values, mapping words to mathematical weights.

03
🤖

AI Prediction

Our pre-trained ML model reads the vectorized data to predict credibility and extract the top trigger words.
